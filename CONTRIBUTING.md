# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in ATHENA.

At this stage, this repository presents the **conceptual public layer** of the framework.

## Scope of contributions

Contributions may be considered for:

- typo corrections,
- formatting improvements,
- clarification suggestions,
- documentation improvements,
- reference suggestions,
- constructive conceptual discussion.

## Out of scope

This repository does not host:

- the private analytical engine,
- internal scoring systems,
- private rule sets,
- confidential operational implementations.

Please do not open pull requests attempting to reconstruct or extrapolate unpublished proprietary components.

## Style

When suggesting edits:

- preserve analytical clarity,
- avoid overstating empirical claims,
- distinguish clearly between conceptual framing and demonstrated evidence,
- keep terminology consistent across files.

## Contact

For research inquiries or collaboration proposals, please use the contact details in `CONTACT.md`.
