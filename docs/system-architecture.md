# System Architecture

## Overview

ATHENA organizes narrative influence analysis around four interacting poles.

| Pole | Function |
|---|---|
| **CORPS** | analyzes the internal structure of the message |
| **TERRAIN** | analyzes the audience’s psychological and cultural disposition |
| **BOUCLIER** | analyzes defensive resistance and reactance |
| **VECTEUR** | analyzes activation and propagation dynamics |

The framework does not treat these poles as isolated variables. Their interaction produces a **configuration**.

That configuration is the main object of ATHENA analysis.

---

## The Four Poles

### CORPS
CORPS examines the internal structure of the narrative, including:

- causal chain
- clarity
- coherence
- emotional congruence
- symbolic and archetypal roles
- framing logic

This pole answers:
**What kind of narrative object is being presented?**

---

### TERRAIN
TERRAIN examines the audience context, including:

- identity proximity
- value compatibility
- cultural resonance
- cognitive availability
- situational tension
- prior interpretive predispositions

This pole answers:
**What kind of audience environment is receiving the message?**

---

### BOUCLIER
BOUCLIER examines resistance, including:

- identity threat
- legitimacy conflict
- perceived manipulation
- autonomy threat
- distrust
- psychological reactance

This pole answers:
**What defensive threshold might the narrative activate?**

BOUCLIER is a core differentiator of ATHENA because reactance is treated here as a **structural analytical pole**, not as a secondary afterthought.

---

### VECTEUR
VECTEUR examines the message’s activation potential, including:

- circulation force
- mobilization energy
- amplification conditions
- social relay potential
- contagiousness of framing
- narrative momentum

This pole answers:
**How is the narrative likely to move, spread, and activate?**

---

## Why the Architecture Matters

The same narrative can produce different outcomes depending on the balance between these poles.

A strong narrative can fail.
A weak narrative can spread.
A technically coherent campaign can generate rejection instead of persuasion.

ATHENA makes these divergences analyzable.
